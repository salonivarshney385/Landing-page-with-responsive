E-learning, or electronic learning, is a type of online education that uses the internet to deliver training, knowledge sharing, and courses. It can be used for a variety of purposes, including academic education, corporate training, and skill development. E-learning is important for both educating students and for professional development. It allows learners to access a wide range of resources independently of time and place.
